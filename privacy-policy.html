Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 1st February 2025

Welcome to Chat IntelCloud (https://chat.intelcloud.ai). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect the data of users who use our platform to manage Messenger conversations via Chatwoot.

1. Information We Collect

We collect only the necessary information required to manage Messenger conversations:

  • Facebook Page ID & Page Name: To connect your Messenger account with Chatwoot.
  • Messenger Conversations: To allow you to manage and respond to messages from customers.
  • Basic User Information: (Facebook User ID & Name) for authentication purposes.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely to enable Messenger chat management on Chat IntelCloud. We do not share or sell your data.

3. Data Security & Retention

We apply strong security measures and do not store messages outside Chatwoot. If you disconnect your Facebook Page, all associated data is removed.

4. Your Rights & Choices

  • You can disconnect your Facebook Page anytime.
  • Request data deletion via info@intelcloud.ai.
  • Revoke access through Facebook Settings.

5. Updates to this Privacy Policy

We may update this policy and will notify users of significant changes.

Contact us: For any inquiries, reach out to us at info@intelcloud.ai.
